Introducing Myself

My name is Ibrahim and I am from Glasgow. I have enjoyed running on and off for the last few years, and I ran the Great Scottish Run 10K with a personal best of 41:24. I have never gone much beyond 12K, but I enjoy big challenges. This time, I want to take the jump to a full marathon for a meaningful cause and raise support for people in need around the world.
