How to Sponsor

A simple guide to reserve a kilometre or contribute to an already reserved KM.

Step by Step

Step 1

Pick a kilometre

Choose any tile from the 42 KM grid. Available tiles can be reserved as the primary sponsor.

Step 2

Submit your details

Complete the form with your name, sponsorship type, and optional message.

Step 3

Use your sponsored KM

After submitting, include your sponsored KM (for example, KM 9) in your JustGiving donation message.

Step 4

Verification

Once payment is confirmed, the admin panel marks your pledge as confirmed on the public tile.

Sponsorship Amounts

  • Primary sponsor: £85 to reserve an available kilometre.
  • Contributors: Any amount can be added to reserved kilometres.
  • Sadaqah Jariyah: Select the option in the form to dedicate a sponsorship.
